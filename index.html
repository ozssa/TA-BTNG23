About Us

Minecraft, a sandbox video game, was created by Swedish programmer Markus "Notch" Persson and was first released in the form of Minecraft Classic in May 2009. Developed and published by Mojang, the game continued to evolve through Alpha and Beta stages before reaching its official release in November 2011. Its rapid popularity led to Microsoft's acquisition of Mojang and the rights to Minecraft in September 2014 for $2.5 billion. Since then, Minecraft has expanded to various platforms and has become more than just a game, influencing popular culture, education, and art, and remains one of the world's most popular games.

Headquarters

The history of Minecraft began with the creation of the game by Markus "Notch" Persson, a Swedish programmer. The game was first released in the form of Minecraft Classic in May 2009. Markus Persson initiated the development of this game independently, and initially, it was just his personal project.

As Minecraft started to gain popularity and a large following, Markus Persson founded a company called Mojang AB in 2010. Mojang's headquarters is located in Stockholm, Sweden. Mojang then took on the responsibility for the development and publishing of Minecraft.
